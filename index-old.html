About

MAKING ENERGY DATA USABLE

Increasingly organisations have access to good quality energy and water usage data from buildings. These data represent an underused resource.

  • Technical aspects - energy management for experts
  • Share information with non-technical people

ECOVISUM is a Leicester-based company committed to helping people and organisations reduce their environmental impact through better management of their energy.

We provide a range of services, specializing in engaging people via energy dashboards and creative visualisations. Most recently we have worked with National Union of Students on their Student Switch-off Campaign.

 

Services

BESPOKE ENERGY-AWARE SOLUTIONS

As a consultancy, we specialize in bespoke innovative solutions for engaging people and organisations with environmental impacts, and offer a bespoke customer focused approach to the design and delivery of innovative solutions designed to help customers reduce their environmental impact.

  • Data analytics and visualisation
  • Web Apps for energy saving
  • Bespoke energy dashboards
  • Energy management
  • Focus groups and user engagement
  • Training and behavior change consultancy
  • Creative visualisations

Research

OUR RESEARCH, ORGANISATIONS AND POLICY MAKERS

Ecovisum directors have undertaken a number of significant projects around energy use and awareness, and published numerous internationally excellent and world leading research papers on the impact and relevance of our work to organisations, academics and policy makers. Further details can be found on our various academic websites at De Montfort University.

DUALL

understanding the role of ICT in reducing energy consumption of a large scale public building through the design of a socio-technical ICT interface connecting building users to their electricity consumption

GREENVIEW

a smart phone webapp that enables building users to understand the energy consumption of the buildings across multiple sites, and post comments with images

GOOD DEEDS

using web technologies to facilitate behaviour change through engaging and empowering employees to reduce the environmental impact of the public buildings in which they work

SAVES

supporting students in minimising their carbon footprint in halls of residence, promoting energy efficiency, and installing good habits which last beyond their time in education

SMART SPACES

a comprehensive approach exploiting the potential of ICT and smart metering for significant energy saving in public buildings, aiming to reduce public sector energy consumption

EDI-NET

a system that provides a thing that helps councils save money by providing live monitoring of the energy use of all their buildings and notifies them of significant changes
